Research

I am broadly interested in developing techniques to solve multimodal problems with real-world constraints of robustness and limited supervision.

At Georgia Tech, I have been exploring parameter-efficient techniques in continual learning settings.

At Facebook AI & USC, I worked on domain generalization and adversarial robustness. I contributed to the development of a novel consistency regularizer that directly operates on loss functions to learn robust and consistent features. Our technique achieved SOTA performance when applied to a variety of unimodal and multimodal tasks.

At CMU, I worked on developing a hierarchical agent for the Vision and Language Navigation in Continuous Environments (VLN-CE) setting that completes long-horizon tasks by decomposing them into easily realizable sub-goals.
