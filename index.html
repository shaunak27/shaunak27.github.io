About

I am a second year Machine Learning PhD student at Georgia Tech advised by Prof. Zsolt Kira. I am broadly interested in vision and language with a focus on learning under continual, open-world distributions.

At Georgia Tech, I have been exploring open-world learning for image and video tasks on an evolving ontology of labels.

Previously, I have worked on domain generalization and robustness in multimodal settings with USC/Meta AI and on embodied visual navigation with CMU.
