Research

My research interests lie in Machine Learning and its applications in Computer Vision and NLP. I have previously worked in the domains of Robustness, Generalization and Multimodality.

At Facebook AI & USC, I contributed to the development of a regularization technique that leverages augmented data to learn robust, domain independent features. Our technique achieved SOTA performance when applied to a variety of tasks involving Domain Generalization, Adversarial Robustness, Multimodal Understanding and Unsupervised Learning.

At CMU, I worked on developing a hierarchical reasoning agent for the Vision and Language Navigation in Continuous Environments (VLN-CE) setting.
